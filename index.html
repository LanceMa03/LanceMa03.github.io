ABOUT ME

I'm a second year student at Purdue University majoring in computer science. Growing up in San Francisco, I've been fascinated with the growing tech industry in front of my eyes; combined with my passion for computers and video games, I knew that I wanted to pursue my degree in computer science. I am currently honing my skills in computer science with courses in Data Structures and Algorithms and Computer Architecture and aspire to become a software engineer after graduation.
